Privacy Policy for Hank Sent Me

Effective Date: June 3, 2025

Hank Sent Me is a Chrome extension that helps users assess the strength of mutual connections on LinkedIn based on visible page content.

What the Extension Does

  • Detects mutual connections on LinkedIn profile and search result pages.
  • Scores the top 10 mutual connections using page visibility and position.
  • Displays the results in a popup interface.
  • All processing is done locally on your device.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect or transmit any personal data.
  • No data is stored remotely or synced.
  • We do not access private messages or LinkedIn APIs.
  • We do not inject or run remote scripts.

Chrome Permissions Requested

Permission Why It Is Needed
activeTab To determine if the current tab is a profile or mutuals page so the extension can behave appropriately.
storage To temporarily store mutual connections while the popup is open.
host_permissions To read public LinkedIn DOM content. No login info or private data is accessed.
