Privacy Policy for Side Reader

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

Side Reader is a Chrome extension that helps actors rehearse lines using AI voice playback. Users can paste dialogue into the extension, choose a voice, and play each line aloud.

What the Extension Does

  • Lets users paste a scene and play it back line-by-line using AI-generated voices.
  • Routes text to a secure proxy server which interacts with ElevenLabs for speech synthesis.
  • Plays back generated audio in the browserâ€”nothing is downloaded or stored automatically.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect or store any personal information or script data.
  • We do not analyze or share what you input into the extension.
  • We do not use any tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
